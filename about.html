Discovery

Just three months before my enrollment in the OSU UX/UI boot camp, the term “User Experience Design” was completely foreign to me. Although I had always craved improvements in the many suboptimal app and web designs I encountered daily, I had never truly imagined myself in the role of a designer. However, once I uncovered what UX/UI designers actually do, I was instantly certain that this was the path I wanted to pursue.

A Little About Me

With an unwavering commitment to learning and improving daily, I approach my hobbies and interests with the same seriousness as my professional endeavors. When not honing my UX skills, I'm immersed in studying Japanese, practicing guitar, or expanding my knowledge on various topics. I'm also a dogsitter who loves photography, and I often practice composition by taking photos, which enhances my design skills as well. As I deepen my understanding of different concepts and disciplines, my decisions grow increasingly nuanced and strategic, fostering more effective growth.
